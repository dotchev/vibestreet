## Reasoning

Friday was a broad sell-off — the worst session since April 2025. A hot May jobs
report (172k vs ~88k expected) revived Fed rate-hike bets, Treasury yields spiked,
and a semiconductor/AI rout (Broadcom failing to raise its AI-chip outlook, a Meta
secondary offering) led the decline. The hike fears hit every sleeve at once, so
even the diversifiers fell:

one volatile session is not a regime change. Overriding the system on its first
stressed week is exactly the undisciplined reaction the strategy is built to avoid.
